About us:

Based right in the heart of the Pearl of Africa, in Kampala, Uganda. Relax logistics tours and travel company offers various services which include; tax advisory services, cargo transportation, clearing and forwarding, motor vehicle registration, branding and design.
We are specialists in tailored tours and travel in Uganda and East Africa. We offer expert services to create unique trips that achieve our clients’ goals and deliver memories that will last a life time. The knowledge and experience required to deliver such high-quality service comes from our love for Africa, its beauty and the joy that comes from helping others share the experience.
Our main focus is to exceed our clients’ expectations and deliver more to make their trip special and we achieve this by focusing on the most exciting destinations and exclusive itineraries, and finest hotels. We offer cultural tours, relaxation tours, trekking tours, special packages and also weekend trips. We always ensure that we deliver the perfect tours for every client.
